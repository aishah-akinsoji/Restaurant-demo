Our Story

Born from a passion
for the extraordinary

Aurum was conceived in 2018 by Executive Chef Isabelle Renard — a vision to dissolve the boundary between restaurant and theatre. Nestled in the heart of Mayfair, our 42-seat dining room offers an unhurried world where seasonal ingredients sourced from trusted British farms meet the refinement of classical French technique.

Every detail — from the hand-thrown ceramics to the curated wine cellar of over 600 labels — is chosen to compose a single, coherent experience. One that stays with you long after the last course.

Before You Arrive

Frequently asked
questions

Demand for Aurum is high, particularly on Friday and Saturday evenings. We recommend reserving your table at least six to eight weeks in advance for weekend dining, and two to three weeks for weeknights. The Chef's Table and Private Dining Room should be booked a minimum of four weeks ahead.

We invite guests to dress elegantly. Smart attire is requested — jackets for gentlemen are welcomed but not mandatory. We simply ask that trainers, sportswear, and casual shorts are not worn in the dining room, so that all guests feel the occasion is respected.

Absolutely. Our kitchen is proud of its ability to accommodate any dietary requirement — vegetarian, vegan, coeliac, and all allergen-related needs — without compromise to the quality or creativity of the experience. Please inform us at time of booking so that we may prepare accordingly.

We ask for 48 hours' notice for any cancellation or amendment. Reservations cancelled within 48 hours are subject to a £50 per person fee. For private dining and Chef's Table bookings, a 72-hour notice period applies. We understand that plans change; please do contact us as early as possible.

Yes. Aurum gift experiences are available for any of our dining formats and can be presented in our hand-crafted gift envelope. They make a considered and memorable gift for birthdays, anniversaries, or any occasion that deserves something beyond ordinary. Please contact our reservations team for details.

Aurum is located at 14 Charles Street, Mayfair, London W1J 5DU, a short walk from Green Park Underground station. We have a partnership with a nearby private parking facility; our reservations team can arrange a parking space upon request. We also recommend our preferred taxi and car service, Meridian, for a seamless arrival.
